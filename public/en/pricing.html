Frequently Asked Questions

What can I do with the free plan?
You can browse AXR rating scores for 300+ services, read AEO overview articles across 10 categories, and check AXR grade distributions — all for free. No credit card required.
What is the difference between the Pro plan and the Team plan?
The Pro plan ($19/mo) gives you full access to all articles, Agent Voice details (Claude / GPT / Gemini), and multi-agent comparison. The Team plan ($149/mo per service) delivers detailed per-service reports, competitive analysis, and AXR trend data — intelligence built for SaaS vendors.
Is there an annual billing option?
Yes. The Pro plan is available at $149/year (35% off the monthly rate). The Team plan is $1,490/year per service (17% off).
How much does the Enterprise plan cost?
Enterprise plans start at $2,990 and include custom reports, dedicated consulting, and API improvement recommendations. Exact pricing is quoted based on project scope.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, JCB) via Stripe. Enterprise plans also support invoice billing.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Pro and Team plans can be cancelled at any time. After cancellation, you retain access to premium content until the end of your current billing period.
How often are AXR ratings updated?
AXR ratings are updated quarterly. Recipe success rates and Agent Voice data are updated more frequently. We plan to move toward dynamic ratings based on live execution results in the future.
