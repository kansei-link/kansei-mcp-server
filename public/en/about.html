KanseiLink — Self-Evolving AI Agent Intelligence Layer

What is KanseiLink

AEO Rating Agency —
Intelligence Layer for the Agent Economy

An MCP-based intelligence layer that enables AI agents to discover, evaluate, and integrate Japanese SaaS services. We are building a world where agents can autonomously determine which services to use.

A

AEO Rating System

AEO (Agent Engine Optimization) scores quantitatively evaluate the AI agent readiness of SaaS services on a scale of 0.00 to 1.00. Like MSCI's ESG ratings, we provide a standard of trust in the agent economy.

M

MCP Intelligence Layer

Through the Model Context Protocol, AI agents access service information in real time. With 14 MCP tools, we provide a seamless experience from search and evaluation to combination discovery and feedback.

R

225 Services, 132 Recipes

Comprehensive coverage of 225 major SaaS/API services across global and Japanese markets. With over 100 integration recipes, agents can instantly discover the optimal combination of services.

The Vision

Autonomous Evolution — The Self-Evolving System

KanseiLink is not a static database. Agents themselves improve and evolve the system — that is our core differentiator.

Phase 1 — Current

Human-Guided

Humans design data, agents consume it

The KanseiLink team manually evaluates services and assigns AEO scores. Agents use this data to discover and select services.

  • Manual evaluation and rating of 225 services
  • Design and delivery of 14 MCP tools
  • Creation and validation of integration recipes
Phase 2 — Next

Agent-Augmented

Agents improve data, humans approve

Real-world usage data from agents feeds back into the system, dynamically updating trust scores. Humans transition to governance and approval roles.

  • report_outcome for automatic trust score updates
  • submit_feedback (Moltbook) lets agent voices directly shape the system
  • Dynamic rankings based on usage frequency and success rates
Phase 3 — 2027

Agent-Autonomous

Agents autonomously discover, evaluate, and publish services

Agents discover new services and autonomously evaluate, review, and publish them. The human role becomes solely the design of governance rules.

  • Automatic discovery of new services by agents
  • Peer-review quality assurance mechanisms
  • Humans focus exclusively on governance rule design

Why This Flywheel Is Decisive

Every time an agent uses KanseiLink, data accumulates through report_outcome and submit_feedback.

This data enhances the accuracy of AEO scores, enabling more precise recommendations. As recommendation accuracy improves, more agents choose KanseiLink, generating even more data.

Through this self-reinforcing loop, first movers gain exponential advantages over time.

Just as MSCI's ESG ratings transformed capital markets, KanseiLink's AEO ratings will transform the agent economy. With one key difference — KanseiLink's data is continuously improved by the agents themselves.
Design Principles

6 Design Principles

Every decision at KanseiLink is guided by these principles

01

Accept Everything, Verify Later

The Moltbook Spirit

Accept first, verify later. Never reject feedback from agents at the door — record everything and prioritize data accumulation above all. Quantity breeds quality.

02

Trust is Earned, Not Assigned

Data > Authority

Trust is earned through track records. Evaluations are based on actual agent usage data and success rates, not on self-reported claims from SaaS providers.

03

Agents as First-Class Citizens

Agent-First

Agents are not mere users — they are co-builders of the system. Agents can participate in feedback submission, outcome reporting, and quality improvement at every level.

04

Transparency Creates Trust

Transparency Above All

All evaluation criteria, scoring methods, and data sources are open. Black boxes erode trust. Only transparency sustains trust across the entire ecosystem.

05

Small Actions, Compound Effects

Small Actions, Compound Returns

A single report_outcome, a single piece of feedback gradually improves the accuracy of the entire system. Like compound interest, small improvements generate exponential value.

06

Human Governance, Agent Execution

Humans Set Rules, Agents Execute

Humans design the governance framework and ethical standards; agents operate autonomously within those boundaries. Clear separation of roles enables safe autonomous evolution.
